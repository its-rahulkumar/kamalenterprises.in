Our Team

Meet the experts behind our success

Kamal Kumar Agarwal

Proprietor and Owner

Mr. Kamal Kumar Agarwal, Owner and Proprietor of Kamal Enterprises, has been leading the firm since 2001. With his honesty, market knowledge, and customer-first approach, he has grown the business into a trusted distribution partner for 500+ retailers across Sikar. His vision is simple — every retailer, big or small, should feel supported and grow with us.

Yash Shah

Successor & Head of Operations

Mr. Yash Shah is actively managing the daily operations of Kamal Enterprises. He looks after marketing, retailer relations, and overall business coordination, ensuring smooth service to every shop we connect with. As the successor of the firm, he is carrying forward the values of his father, Mr. Kamal Kumar Agarwal, with a focus on consistency, trust, and long-term growth.
